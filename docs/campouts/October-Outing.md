# October Outing — Wakefield Shooting Sports

## Trip Details

<div class="report-meta" markdown>

**Who:** Troop 364
**What:** Annual Shooting Sports Weekend
**Where:** Wakefield, Virginia
**When:** October 10–12
**Why:** Learn firearm safety, target shooting, and archery

---

For our annual October outing we go to Wakefield to learn how to shoot safely. We spend the weekend shooting at targets, learning different calibers, and also trying bows and other equipment.

### Activities
- :material-pistol: Rifle shooting
- :material-bullseye-arrow: Archery
- Shotgun
- Various caliber training
- Firearm safety instruction
