## Weather

Thursday Morning: Cloudy when we arrived at the boat landing and loaded the canoes, started to rain half way to the island. The wind added an extra challenge to canoeing to the island, with all our gear.

Thursday Afternoon: Cleared up and temperatures were in the mid 80's

Thursday Night: about an inch or rain. Temps in the mid 60's.

Friday: Overcast and hot, high 80's. Great for water-skiing

Saturday: Sunny and hot, high 80's, may have touched the low 90's. Great for water-skiing

Sunday: Sunny and hot, high 80's.
