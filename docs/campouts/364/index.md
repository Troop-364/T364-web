# Troop 364 Campout Reports

Welcome to our campout reports! After each outing, scouts record what they experienced — weather, wildlife, plants, gear lessons, and food. This is our outdoor journal as a troop.

## How We Document Outings

Each campout report follows a standard template so scouts can practice **naturalist observation skills**. Reports include:

- :material-weather-partly-cloudy: **Weather** – What was the weather like?
- :material-bird: **Wildlife** – Animals spotted during the trip
- :material-tree: **Plants & Trees** – Flora identified in the area
- :material-mushroom: **Mushrooms & Fungi** – Any interesting fungi found
- :material-tent: **Gear** – What we wish we brought (and what we wish we left behind)
- :material-pot-steam: **Food** – Meals cooked at camp
- :material-lightbulb: **Lessons Learned** – Tips for next time
- :material-shield-account: **Patrols** – Which patrols participated
