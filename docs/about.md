# About Troop 364 & 5364

## Our History

Troop 364 was established in **1968** in Virginia Beach, Virginia, as part of the Tidewater Council of the Boy Scouts of America. For over 55 years, our troop has helped young men develop outdoor, leadership, and survival skills in a **boy-run, boy-led** setting.

Our scouts learn skills that help them succeed not just in scouting, but in everyday life — skills they may not learn anywhere else.

## What We Do

Our troop stays active year-round with monthly campouts and weekly meetings. Here's a taste of what we do:

| Activity | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| **Camping** | Monthly campouts at parks, reserves, and scout camps |
| **Hiking** | Old Rag, Three Ridges (15 miles!), and local trails |
| **Water Sports** | Canoeing, paddleboarding, wakeboarding at Hemeric Island |
| **Shooting Sports** | Rifle, shotgun, and archery at Wakefield |
| **Merit Badges** | 125+ earned at Summer Camp 2024 alone! |
| **Community Service** | Building bat houses, benches, and camp improvements |
| **Winter Camping** | Klondike Derby prep, Camp Brady Saunders |
| **Summer Camp** | A week at Camp Shenandoah every July |
