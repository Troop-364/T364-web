# Gear and Random Checklists

Just one big list of potential gear to take camping, this is not specific to any campout. Don't go out and buy all this. This is just one big long list.



## Basic Sleeping Gear

- tent
- tent stakes
- ground tarp
- sleeping bag
- pillow

### Other Sleeping Gear

- foldable lightweight hiking cot
- hammock

## Hammock Camping

- hammock
- hammock under-quilt
- hammock rain tarp
- mosquitoes net

## Safety

- sun screen
- bug spray for ticks and mosquitos
- hat
- water bottle
- poison ivy soap
- sun glasses
- work gloves
- eye protection for certain activities like splitting wood


## Fire

- first aid kit
- Matches
- ferro rod
- lighter
- candles
- dryer lint
- kindle cracker
- pocket knife
- hatchet
- axe
- pickaroon

## Eating

- fork, knife, spoon
- mess kit
- hot-coco cup
- water bottle

## Toiletries

- soap
- wash cloth
- towel
- tooth paste
- tooth brush
- shower shoes

## Hot Weather
 
- hydration packets 
- nice wide brimmed hat
- loose fitting shirt
- hip fan - rechargeable
- tent fan - rechargeable
- ice chest
- water shoes
- cloths line

## Random

- solar charger
- radios
- power strip for AC
- extension cord
- laser cutter

- Laundry soap and washing bucket - optional luxury
