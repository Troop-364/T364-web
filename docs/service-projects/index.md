# Service Project Reports

Our troop serves the community through service projects throughout the year. Use this section to record where we served, what we did, who participated, and what we learned.

!!! tip "Scouts: Want to add a new report?"
    Copy the [Service Project Template](TEMPLATE.md) and fill in your project details. Use first names, nicknames, or patrol names only.

---

## How We Document Service Projects

Each service project report follows a standard template so scouts can record the important details in one place:

- **Project details** - What we did, where we served, and when
- **Participation** - Which scouts, patrols, and adults helped
- **Service impact** - Who benefited and how
- **Hours** - Time spent by the troop
- **Lessons learned** - Notes for future projects
- **Photos** - Optional pictures from the project
