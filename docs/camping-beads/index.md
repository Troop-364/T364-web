# Camping Beads

Camping beads are a long tradition in scouting. They are a physical representation of the number of days a scout camped and the weather of the campout.

- green - fair weather
- blue - rain
- white - snow
- red - over 90
- purple - below 32 and no snow


We added a twist to the standard beads. We cut leather tags, with a laser cutter and attach the beads to the leather. Attached is the template.
