# Camping Beads

Camping beads are a long tradition in scouting. They are a physical representation of the number of days a scout camped and the weather of the campout.

## Bead Colors

| Color | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Green | Fair weather |
| Blue | Rain |
| White | Snow |
| Red | Over 90 F |
| Purple | Below 32 F and no snow |

## Our Version

We added a twist to the standard beads. We cut leather tags with a laser cutter and attach the beads to the leather.
